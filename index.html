Game of Life Visualizer

This application is a tribute to the late Jon Conway and his Game of Life.

For more information on the Game of Life check out the Wikipedia page for it here.

Here is a quick tutorial for your convenience:

(1) You can click to select individual grid squares or you can click and drag to select multiple squares at once.

(2) The X button will clear the board and the play/pause button will start and pause the simulation.
